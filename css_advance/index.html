Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TechnoFair 2025

When and where is TechnoFair 2025?

+

TechnoFair 2025 will take place on March 15–17, 2025 at the Moscone Center in San Francisco, California.

Are tickets refundable?

+

Yes! We offer a full refund if you cancel up to 30 days before the event. After that, tickets are transferable.

Will talks be recorded?

+

All main stage talks will be professionally recorded and made available to attendees within 2 weeks after the event.

Is there a code of conduct?

+

Yes, we follow a strict Code of Conduct to ensure a safe and inclusive environment for everyone.

Can I volunteer or speak?

+

Absolutely! Speaker applications are closed for 2025, but volunteer sign-ups open in January. Stay tuned!

What COVID-19 safety measures are in place?

+

We follow local health guidelines. Proof of vaccination or negative test may be required. Full policy will be emailed 2 weeks before the event.
